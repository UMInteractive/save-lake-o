About Us

In collaboration with the Audubon Society in Florida, Save Lake O is a project aimed at raising awareness and communicating implementable solutions to the persistent algal blooms problem. By collaborating as a community and educating people about the cause and impact of algal blooms, we believe that everyone can contribute and be a part of the cleaner future initiative that will help protect the habitats, wildlife, people and environment.
